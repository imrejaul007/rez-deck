Why REZ Can't Be Copied

Behavioral Data Ownership

AGGREGATORS OWN THE TRANSACTION. REZ OWNS THE RELATIONSHIP.

This data is: Not available anywhere else, growing with every transaction, improving every AI model, locked in by habit loops.

Cross-Category Intelligence

REZ MULTI-CATEGORY: "We know how people live." Cross-merchant patterns. Rich prediction power. Strong habit formation. Real behavioral graph.

Local Density Lock-in
